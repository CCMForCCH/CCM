Pizza/Potluck Directions


ORGANIZING A MONDAY COMMUNITY PIZZA POTLUCK MEAL

Ultimately the team leader(s) are responsible! Ensure all attendees are listed on CCM, invite people with birthdays to attend and celebrate, arrange for birthday cake (families often provide). Leaders announce the meal and who or what it is celebrating to community, establish the sign-ups, closing meal sign-up at 6 PM on Sunday. Place pizza order with American Flat Bread Pizza taking into account peopleâ€™s wish and health needs, put tab and tip on own credit card, identify who picks up by 5:45 PM, for 6 PM meal. It is the leader(s) job to stay within a budget: multiply the number of attendees paying for pizza by $10 to get total budget for pizza meal maybe including cake etc. By community agreement kids count as half a sign-up but CCM cannot do half.


Ensure enough food for gathering, create a welcome atmosphere, ensure sanitary procedures plus 2 hour safe-handling rule are followed, get pizza, hand receipts and accounting to Marianne, wipe kitchen counters and tables, wash dishes and put away, wash linens and put away, put away or send away all food, compost and trash and recycle each bagged and deposited in the community collection bins, the floor cleaned of food bits, outside door locked and windows closed, the lights off. Then remember the thank-yous and maybe set up the next pizza potluck!


Enforce the understanding that everyone attending contributes to provisioning the meal either by helping pay a share of the pizzas (about $10.00 each when Dan issues bills) or bringing a potluck contribution of about 6-8 servings. People may bring their own meal, but we request they also contribute to the community meal by pay or potluck!)


BUILD A TEAM TO MAKE THIS MORE FUN!

Here are some tasks easily delegated:

  • Water pitchers: filled, refrigerated, and delivered to each table by 6 PM
  • Tables and chairs: arranged with enough seating not too close together for the group by 5:45 PM
  • Pick up pizza order: take receipt, American Flat Bread Pizza, 45 Day Street Somerville at 5:45 PM
  • Set up tables: with tablecloths, and serving table with pads for hot plates, wipe tables after meal, and if tablecloths, etc, if needed, wash and dry in machines and replace in kitchen
  • Organize: as delivered, listing of red-flag ingredients for potluck, and/or whom to ask for info
  • Host Event: check servers for potlucks, desserts stay in kitchen until after announcements, introductions. Hosting team is thanked by name and applauded, and birthday candles lit and happy birthdays sung, cake cut. Greet and support individuals
  • Dishwasher loaded: pots, pitchers, serving dishes, utensils washed and put away then or next day.
  • Outside: close umbrellas, nothing left on tables, door locked! No food left outside or inside
  • Indoors: If people forget to take potluck, phone or compost, nothing remains in dining room or kitchen, stove off, windows closed, no food or trash or compost left, lights off! No food left out!
